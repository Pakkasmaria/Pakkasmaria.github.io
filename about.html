Hello there!

I'm a self taught artist with many areas of interest. I've spent my whole life drawing and running in the woods playing in my imaginary worlds, and I dream of spreading the love and appreciation for nature and animals to other people, most important of them all my little niece.

When I get my head wrapped around a project I literally cannot think anything else for a couple of weeks, be it animating, drawing a deck of cards or writing a book.
